How We're Doing

Key Performance Indicators

BART tracks hundreds of performance measures at all levels of the organization. The most important measures, are organized according to eight Strategic Plan Goals. The percentage of satisfied customers with BART has dropped recently due to reliability problems, crowding, and other issues. Similarly, on-time performance has fallen recently due to aging infrastructure, new safety rules, and increased ridership. There are multiple initiatives such as planned system investments, including new train cars, escalator/elevator overhauls, and implementation of Measure RR projects should help to improve customer service over time.
